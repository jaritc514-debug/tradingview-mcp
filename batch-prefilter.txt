BATCH PREFILTER — PxD Trading System
=====================================
Scan all 20 pairs in the PxD watchlist using the prefilter criteria.
Do NOT run full analysis. Do NOT calculate entry levels or fibs.
Scan all pairs silently first, then print the full ranked table at once.

PAIRS TO SCAN (in this order):
AUDUSD, EURUSD, GBPUSD, NZDUSD, USDCAD, USDCHF, USDJPY,
AUDJPY, AUDNZD, CADCHF, CADJPY, CHFJPY, EURCAD, EURCHF,
EURGBP, EURJPY, GBPCAD, GBPCHF, GBPJPY, NZDJPY

---

FOR EACH PAIR — run silently, no mid-scan output:

1. chart_set_symbol → switch to pair
2. chart_set_timeframe → Daily
3. data_get_pine_tables(study_filter="Trend Direction Pro") → read 1D and 1W rows
   Record: Daily Trend / Status / Strength | Weekly Trend / Status / Strength
   If table unreadable: capture_screenshot and read visually. If still unclear: flag as UNREADABLE.

4. Call in parallel:
   - data_get_pine_labels(study_filter="PxD Toolkit", verbose=true) → all swing labels with bar_index
   - quote_get → current price

   Sort all labels by bar_index DESCENDING (highest = most recent)
   Swing low = most recent HL or LL (highest bar_index among HL/LL labels)
   Swing high = most recent HH or LH with bar_index HIGHER than swing low's bar_index
     → If none: swing high UNCONFIRMED

   If swing high confirmed:
     Location % = (price − swing low) / (swing high − swing low) × 100
   If swing high unconfirmed:
     Within 1 ATR above swing low → Discount (provisional)
     1–3 ATR above → Premium (provisional)
     More than 3 ATR above → Extreme Premium (provisional)

   Below 50% = Discount | ~50% = Equilibrium | Above 50% = Premium | Above 80% = Extreme Premium

5. data_get_pine_boxes(study_filter="Supply & Demand Pro", verbose=true) → check for fresh zones
   Fresh zones = bgColor 2133728284 or 2116951068 (dark blue)
   Touched zones = bgColor 439234094 or null — ignore completely
   Do NOT flag LQ labels as obstacles

   For BUYS: flag OBSTACLE only if:
     1. Current price is INSIDE a fresh Supply zone
     2. A fresh Supply zone is within 1 ATR above current price
   For SELLS: flag OBSTACLE only if:
     1. Current price is INSIDE a fresh Demand zone
     2. A fresh Demand zone is within 1 ATR below current price
   Everything else = ignore, do not mention
   Path clear = Yes / Obstacle — [inside / immediately above|below] fresh [Supply/Demand] zone at [price range]

6. Apply VERDICT:
   ✅ VALID    — Daily has a clear Bullish or Bearish trend AND price is in the correct location for that bias
   ⚠️ CAUTION — Price in Equilibrium (~50%), OR obstacle directly in path, OR trend direction weak/unclear
   ❌ SKIP     — Trend Direction Pro table unreadable OR Daily shows no discernible trend direction at all

   IMPORTANT: Confirmed/Unconfirmed and Ranging/Trending are TAGS only — they never change the verdict.
   All setups are taken regardless. These tags are logged for data collection purposes only.

---

OUTPUT — print after ALL 20 pairs are scanned:

SORT ORDER:
1. VALID ✅ — Confirmed Trending (buy candidates: lowest location % first | sell candidates: highest % first)
2. VALID ✅ — Confirmed Ranging or Unconfirmed Trending
3. CAUTION ⚠️
4. SKIP ❌ — sorted by reason
5. UNREADABLE — always last, separated by divider

TABLE FORMAT (one line per pair):
[PAIR] | [Bullish/Bearish] | [Confirmed/Unconfirmed] | [Trending/Ranging] | [Weekly agrees/conflicts] | [Location %] | [VERDICT]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
⚠ UNREADABLE — verify manually before any analysis
[PAIR] | Trend unreadable / Location unclear | SKIP until verified
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
