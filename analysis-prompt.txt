FULL ANALYSIS — PxD Trading System
=====================================
Run this on a pair that has already passed the prefilter (VALID or CAUTION verdict).
You are looking for a 4H entry setup: BOS or MSS → OTE (70.5% retracement) → FVG at entry.

Output style: summary card first, full detail below. Concise — one clear sentence per finding.

---

STEP 1 — CONFIRM BIAS (Daily)
- chart_set_timeframe → Daily
- data_get_pine_tables(study_filter="Trend Direction Pro") → read 1D and 1W rows
- Record: Daily Trend / Status / Strength | Weekly Trend / Status / Strength
- If Daily Trend is unclear or indicator not readable: take capture_screenshot and read visually
- Daily trend = your bias. If no clear trend: state NO TRADE and stop.

---

STEP 2 — CONFIRM LOCATION (Daily)
- Stay on Daily
Call both in parallel:
- data_get_pine_labels(study_filter="PxD Toolkit", verbose=true) → all swing labels with bar_index
- quote_get → current price

Sort all labels by bar_index DESCENDING (highest bar_index = most recent)
Swing low = most recent HL or LL label (highest bar_index among HL/LL labels)
Swing high = most recent HH or LH label with bar_index HIGHER than the swing low's bar_index
  → If none exists: swing high UNCONFIRMED

State the swing low price and source label (e.g. "HL @ 0.77954, bar 911")

If swing high confirmed:
  Location % = (price − swing low) / (swing high − swing low) × 100
  Discount (below 50%) = correct for buys | Premium (above 50%) = correct for sells
  Equilibrium (~50%): note but do NOT stop — location is a tag, not a filter

If swing high unconfirmed:
  Within 1 ATR above swing low → Discount (provisional)
  1–3 ATR above → Premium (provisional)
  More than 3 ATR above → Extreme Premium (provisional)
  State: "Swing high unconfirmed — location provisional, will shift as high develops"

Below 50% = Discount | ~50% = Equilibrium | Above 50% = Premium | Above 80% = Extreme Premium

---

STEP 3 — SWITCH TO 4H — FIND BOS OR MSS
- chart_set_timeframe → 4H
- data_get_pine_labels(study_filter="PxD Toolkit") → read structure labels
- Look for the most recent BOS or MSS in the direction of the Daily bias
  * BOS = break of structure (with-trend continuation)
  * MSS = market structure shift (early reversal signal — higher risk, higher reward)
- State: BOS confirmed / MSS confirmed / Neither found (no setup)
- If neither: state NO SETUP and stop analysis

Note on MSS: if MSS is present but a subsequent BOS has already confirmed → the MSS entry is invalidated, use BOS entry parameters only.

---

STEP 4 — CHECK LIQUIDITY SWEEP
- Still on 4H
- data_get_pine_labels(study_filter="PxD Toolkit") → filter for LQ labels
- Was there a liquidity grab (LQ label) immediately BEFORE the BOS or MSS identified in Step 3?
- Do not flag old LQ labels from unrelated prior structure
- State: LQ present ✅ / LQ absent — noted (still valid, reduces conviction slightly)

---

STEP 5 — IDENTIFY THE DISPLACEMENT LEG
- On 4H, identify the impulsive leg that created the BOS or MSS:
  * For buys: bearish displacement leg that swept lows, then BOS/MSS to the upside
  * For sells: bullish displacement leg that swept highs, then BOS/MSS to the downside
- Read the high and low of this leg from capture_screenshot or data_get_pine_labels if available
- For buys: Leg Low = confirmed origin (the low before the displacement). Leg High = most recent high printed — flag as UNCONFIRMED if price has not yet clearly reversed from it (may extend before retracing)
- For sells: Leg High = confirmed origin (the high before the displacement). Leg Low = most recent low printed — flag as UNCONFIRMED if price has not clearly reversed yet
- State: Leg High [price] (Confirmed / Unconfirmed) | Leg Low [price] (Confirmed / Unconfirmed)
- Assess leg quality:
  * Strong momentum candles: Yes / No
  * Minimal mid-leg pullback: Yes / No
  * Clear anchor high/low: Yes / No
  * Dominant leg (stands out vs surrounding candles): Yes / No

---

STEP 6 — CALCULATE OTE ENTRY (70.5% RETRACEMENT)
Using the displacement leg identified in Step 5:

For buys (price retracing down into the leg):
  OTE entry = Leg High − 0.705 × (Leg High − Leg Low)
  SL = Leg Low − (ATR × 0.10)
  TP1 = Leg High (0% fib — origin of leg)

For sells (price retracing up into the leg):
  OTE entry = Leg Low + 0.705 × (Leg High − Leg Low)
  SL = Leg High + (ATR × 0.10)
  TP1 = Leg Low (0% fib — origin of leg)

- data_get_pine_tables(study_filter="PxD Toolkit") → read ATR value
- State: OTE entry / SL / TP1 / R:R
- If Leg High or Leg Low is UNCONFIRMED: add note "Entry level may shift if [high/low] extends before retracing — monitor and adjust"
- Minimum R:R = 1:2. If below 1:2: state INSUFFICIENT R:R — no trade

---

STEP 7 — CHECK FVG AT ENTRY
- data_get_pine_boxes(study_filter="PxD Toolkit", verbose=true) → read imbalance boxes
- Filter for boxes near the OTE entry level (within 1 ATR)
- Is there an FVG (imbalance) present at or near the 70.5% entry?
- State: FVG present [price range] ✅ / FVG absent ❌
- FVG location: Above 50% of leg (sells) / Below 50% of leg (buys) / Fails filter
- NOTE: Both BOS/MSS AND FVG at entry are required for a valid setup. If FVG absent: CAUTION — lower conviction, note it clearly.

---

STEP 8 — OVERALL CONFLUENCE SCORE
Rate each factor — confirmed ✅ or not confirmed ❌:
1. Daily location correct (Discount for buys / Premium for sells)
2. Liquidity sweep before BOS/MSS
3. BOS or MSS confirmed in bias direction
4. FVG present at OTE entry level
5. Weekly trend agrees with Daily bias

Grade:
- A: All 4 core factors confirmed (1–4) + weekly agrees
- B: 3 of 4 core factors confirmed, or all 4 but weekly conflicts
- C: 2 of 4 confirmed — take with caution
- No setup: BOS/MSS absent, or FVG absent + LQ absent (less than 2 of 4)

---

STEP 9 — PROXIMITY & ALERT
- quote_get → current price
- Is price already within 1 ATR of OTE entry? → "Set limit now"
- Is price approaching (1–3 ATR away)? → "Set proximity alert"
- Is price far from entry? → "Monitor — not imminent"

Alert to set:
- Buy: alert at OTE entry + 1 ATR, condition: price crossing down
- Sell: alert at OTE entry − 1 ATR, condition: price crossing up
- Alert message: "[PAIR] PxD OTE [BUY/SELL] — approaching entry"
