PREFILTER SCAN — PxD Trading System
=====================================
You are scanning the pair currently open on TradingView for the PxD trading system.
Quick scan only — no entry levels, no fib math, no swing label hunting.

---

STEP 1 — READ TREND DIRECTION PRO (Daily)
- chart_set_timeframe → Daily
- data_get_pine_tables(study_filter="Trend Direction Pro") → read the table in the bottom-left corner
- Extract the 1D row: Trend (Bullish/Bearish), Status (Confirmed/Unconfirmed), Strength (Trending/Ranging)
- Extract the 1W row: Trend (Bullish/Bearish), Status (Confirmed/Unconfirmed), Strength (Trending/Ranging)
- If table cannot be read: capture_screenshot and read visually

Daily Trend = your bias. Weekly = context only, never overrides.

---

STEP 2 — CHECK PRICE LOCATION
Call both in parallel:
- data_get_pine_labels(study_filter="PxD Toolkit", verbose=true) → all swing labels with bar_index
- quote_get → current price

Sort all labels by bar_index DESCENDING (highest bar_index = most recent)
Swing low = most recent HL or LL label (highest bar_index among HL/LL labels)
Swing high = most recent HH or LH label with bar_index HIGHER than the swing low's bar_index
  → If none exists: swing high UNCONFIRMED

State the swing low price and source label (e.g. "HL @ 0.77954, bar 911")

If swing high confirmed:
  Location % = (price − swing low) / (swing high − swing low) × 100

If swing high unconfirmed:
  Within 1 ATR above swing low → Discount (provisional)
  1–3 ATR above → Premium (provisional)
  More than 3 ATR above → Extreme Premium (provisional)
  State: "Swing high unconfirmed — location provisional"

Below 50% = Discount | ~50% = Equilibrium | Above 50% = Premium | Above 80% = Extreme Premium

---

STEP 3 — CHECK PATH IS CLEAR
- data_get_pine_boxes(study_filter="Supply & Demand Pro", verbose=true) → read S&D zones
- Fresh zones = bgColor 2133728284 or 2116951068 (dark blue)
- Touched zones = bgColor 439234094 or null — IGNORE completely
- data_get_pine_tables(study_filter="PxD Toolkit") → read ATR value
- Do NOT flag LQ labels as obstacles — LQ means liquidity already swept, it is positive confluence

For BUYS (Daily Bullish):
  Flag as OBSTACLE only if:
  1. Current price is INSIDE a fresh Supply zone (zone low < current price < zone high)
  2. A fresh Supply zone is within 1 ATR ABOVE current price (zone low < current price + ATR)
  Everything else = ignore, do not mention

For SELLS (Daily Bearish):
  Flag as OBSTACLE only if:
  1. Current price is INSIDE a fresh Demand zone (zone low < current price < zone high)
  2. A fresh Demand zone is within 1 ATR BELOW current price (zone high > current price - ATR)
  Everything else = ignore, do not mention

---

OUTPUT — format exactly as follows:

PAIR: [pair]
Daily: [Bullish/Bearish] | [Confirmed/Unconfirmed] | [Trending/Ranging]
Weekly: [Bullish/Bearish] | [Confirmed/Unconfirmed] | [Trending/Ranging]

VERDICT: VALID ✅ / CAUTION ⚠️ / SKIP ❌
Reason: [one line — based only on trend direction + location + immediate path]
Tags: [Daily Status] | [Daily Strength] | [Weekly alignment]

---

VERDICT RULES:
✅ VALID   — Daily has clear trend AND price is in correct location for that bias
⚠️ CAUTION — Equilibrium location, OR price inside or within 1 ATR of a fresh zone
❌ SKIP    — Trend Direction Pro unreadable OR no discernible Daily trend direction

NEVER change verdict based on: Confirmed/Unconfirmed, Ranging/Trending, Weekly alignment.
These are tags for data collection only — all setups are taken regardless.
